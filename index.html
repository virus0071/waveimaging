Quantity



Manufacturer: GE
Width: 544 mm, 21 3/4″
Depth: 844 mm, 33 1/4″
Height: 1230 mm – 1670 mm, 48 3/8″ – 65 3/4″ (up/down mechanism + LCD arm)
Weight: 126 kg , 278 lbs
Nominal input voltage: 100-240 VAC, 50/60 Hz
Typical power consumption: 500 W @ default cardiac preset with M5Sc
Rated power consumption: 700 W

Description

Brand new GE E90 is a premium cardiac ultrasound machine. The Vivid E90includes the advanced software-based beamformer cSound instead of outdated hardware based systems. cSound created increased performance in near field image quality, lateral wall definition, image uniformity, and penetration.

Probe Technology

The XDclear™ series of probes are designed to help deliver powerful and efficient sound waves, with high bandwidth and efficiency. The XDclear probe technology provides impressive deep penetration and high sensitivity while maintaining high spatial resolution. The combination of Single Crystal, Acoustic Amplifier and Cool Stack technologies is the core technology of the XDclear series of probes.
